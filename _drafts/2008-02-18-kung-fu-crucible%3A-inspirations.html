Nixon run a session of his yet-to-be-released game, Inuma.  It made a strong impression on me. Part of the game is making a large fictional world as a group, that your later stories will explore, and there's a specific order of operations that the procedure follows. The first step is always to list the new world's inspirations: the media, whether movies, books, shows, music or games, which you want the new world to be able to claim as an influence.

It struck me as a good idea for reasons I can't fully explain. Here follows a list of things which I want to be semantically connected to this my hypothetical game/setting.
