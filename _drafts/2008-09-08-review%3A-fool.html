--- layout: post title: 'Review: Fool' date: '2008-09-08T20:10:00.003-04:00' author: Nick Novitski tags: - gaming modified_time: '2008-11-11T14:05:58.932-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-31707300.post-8832699948077322699 --- Fool is a game for three to five (or so) people, played using a regular deck of cards.  It's Russian.  Or at least, I learned it from a Russian, and they claimed the game was also Russian.  Who can say for certain?

Set-Up:

Deal six cards to each player.  Then draw one more card and put it face up in the center of the table.  It's still part of the deck, though...you may want to put it at the bottom, sideways, so everyone can see its value and also draw cards from the deck.
