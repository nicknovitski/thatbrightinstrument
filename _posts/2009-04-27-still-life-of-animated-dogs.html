I'm not going to be able to justify the expense of owning a dog for a long time yet, despite, you know, loving dogs.

Dogs don't love unconditionally, but they're sociable, and they don't seem complex enough for self-deception. And certainly, I think this movie reminded me why it can be good to have a pet like that.
