--- layout: post title: In a Grove date: '2008-01-16T19:49:00.001-05:00' author: Nick Novitski tags: - in a grove - fiction - gaming - writing modified_time: '2008-08-20T20:17:43.386-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-31707300.post-1164921984043080421 blogger_orig_url: http://brightinstrument.blogspot.com/2008/01/in-grove.html --- I've authored a Neverwinter Nights module based on Akira Kurosawa's movie Rashomon. I have done this for two reasons.
  1. I was applying to a writer's position at Bioware Austin, and they needed an interactive writing sample using NWN.
  2. I was in a preceptorial class on Kurosawa, where one option for our final writing project was to produce a treatment for a remake of one of his films.
I owe immense thanks to my tutor for permitting and fostering this project. I will owe immense thanks to Bioware if they play it and hire me. Fingers crossed!

If you have an updated copy of Neverwinter Nights, please feel free to download my module and tell me what you think.
